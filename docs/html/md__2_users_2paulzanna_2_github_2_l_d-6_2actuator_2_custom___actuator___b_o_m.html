This document complements the specifications by providing a dimensioned sketch pack and a bill of materials (BOM) for manufacturing and CAD integration.

1. Sketch Pack

1.1 Overall Assembly

  • Outer Diameter (OD): 95 mm (Unitree standard)
  • Wall Thickness: 2 mm
  • Depth: 42 mm total
  • Clearance: ≥30 mm inner bore for cycloidal gearset

1.2 Exploded View

Components (from outside to inside):

  1. Aluminium Housing (95 mm OD, 2 mm wall)
  2. Rotor Ring with NdFeB magnets (85 mm OD)
  3. Stator core with segmented laminated teeth
  4. Windings (copper, enamel coated)
  5. Dual angular contact bearings
  6. Central shaft (hardened steel)
  7. Cycloidal gearbox (double-disc, anti-backlash)
  8. Output plate and fasteners

1.3 Sectional Views

  • Rotor-Stator Clearance: 0.5–1 mm airgap
  • Stator Stack Length: 27–30 mm
  • Cycloidal Gear Thickness: 6–8 mm discs
  • Bearing Seats: 10–15 mm width, tolerance H7

3. Notes for CAD Integration

  • Keep bearing alignment surfaces concentric within ±0.02 mm.
  • Rotor balance is critical — use epoxy or balancing weights.
  • Cycloidal pins require H7 tolerance bores to ensure longevity.
  • Use spline-compatible shaft/output plate if reusing Unitree joints.
